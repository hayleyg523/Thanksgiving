Why Am I So Thankful For My Friends?

When it comes to my friends, they are my everything. I really couldn't be anymore thankful for them. Whether its inside or outside of school, I am always laughing annd having a great time with them in any circumstance. They always know how to put a smile on my face and they make it a whole lot easier to get through any day. I am so lucky to have so many amazing friends who support me and are there for me whenever I need them. They all hold such a strong place in my heart. On Thanksgiving, I think about how lucky I am to have these types of individuals in my life who I have nothing but care and love towards. My friends help to make life better and make it easier to journey through the hard times.
